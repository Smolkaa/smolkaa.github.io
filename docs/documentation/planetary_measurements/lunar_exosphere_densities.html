Noble Gases

Hydrogen Based Species

Atomic Hydrogen – \(\ce{H}\)

Table 1: Caption
Description Value \(\left[\,\mathrm{cm^{\mathrm{-3}}}\right]\) Reference(s)
upper limit \(1.7 \times 10^1\) Gladstone et al. (2009)
LAMP \(1.4 \times 10^{-1}\) Gladstone et al. (2009)
LAMP, upper limit \(2.4 \times 10^1\) Cook et al. (2013)

Gladstone et al. (2009) assume for the derivations a \(30\,\mathrm{min}\), \(3\sigma\) observation, and a path length of \(300\,\mathrm{km}\).

Molecular Hydrogen – \(\ce{H2}\)

Table 2: Caption
Description Value \(\left[\,\mathrm{cm^{\mathrm{-3}}}\right]\) Reference(s)
Model predictions, subsolar \(2.0 \times 10^3\) Hodges, Hoffman, and Johnson (1973)
Model predictions, antisolar \(1.2 \times 10^4\) Hodges, Hoffman, and Johnson (1973)

Hodges, Hoffman, and Johnson (1973) assumed a solar wind proton influx of \(3 \times 10^8 \,\mathrm{s^{\mathrm{-1}}cm^{\mathrm{-3}}}\) for their simulations.
